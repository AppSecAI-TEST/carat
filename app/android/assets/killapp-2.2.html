How to Kill / Restart an App

From Settings, Choose Applications or Apps. Click the Running tab. You should see something like this:

If necessary, scroll up or down to locate the app you want to close. When you've found it, click it. You should see something like this:

Press Force Stop or Stop. Once you are done killing apps, press the home button to close the App Manager. If you were trying to restart the app, now simply open it again, as usual.
